ingestion schema (version 1)

Document
├── document_id        (UUID)
├── source_type        (pdf | docx | text | url | manual)
├── source_name        (filename / url / user label)
├── raw_text           (original extracted text)
├── created_at
├── ingestion_status   (PENDING | INGESTED | FAILED)
├── checksum           (for deduplication)
